    Sojan Prajapati is an entrepreneur and technology professional working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, business innovation, and storytelling. He serves as the Chief Business Officer of Ting Ting, Nepal’s first AI-powered programmable voice telephony system, where he focuses on product strategy, partnerships, and market growth to transform how businesses communicate with customers. Alongside his work in technology, Sojan is the creator and host of Kathmandu Ko Katha, a storytelling platform dedicated to sharing the history, culture, and lesser-known narratives of Kathmandu. Through this dual work in innovation and cultural documentation, he represents a new generation of Nepali entrepreneurs blending technology with creative expression.
